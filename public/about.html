How It Works

Kryon Stage uses a unique 5-level progression system that mirrors real idol training:

Level 1: Trainee

Learn the basics with text-based choreography descriptions. Build foundation skills.

Level 2: Rookie

Express creativity through emoji and ASCII art performances. Develop unique style.

Level 3: Rising Star

Master signature moves and build brand identity. Create memorable performances.

Level 4: Pro Idol

Execute professional timestamped shows with precision timing and complex choreography.

Level 5: Legend

Generate AI-ready visual performances. Achieve legendary status and inspire others.
